Baby Pictures

Taylor Alison Swift, born December 13 1989 in Pennsylvania, later moving to Tennessee grew up with a passion for music. At the age of 14 she pursued her passion and became a country singer.

Primary School

A young 9 year old Taylor attended West reading Elementary Center and Wyomissing Area Junior/Senior Highschool. This young Taylor had a passion for music and won her first talent show at age 11.

High School

As Taylor's career took off at such an early age, she didn't get to experience a normal highschool life. She studied for 2 years at Hendersonville Highschool before transferring to Aaron academy. Doing so allowed her to be homeschooled while touring

Family

Taylor's parents, Archie Dean (Father) and Rose Baldi Swift (Mother) have been supportive of her career since young. Taylor also has a brother called Austin Swift.

Taylor + Pets

Taylor has 3 cats calleld Meredith Grey, Olivia Benson and Benjamin Button.

Awards

Taylor has won many awards for her music some of which include, Academy of Country Music Awards, American Country Awards, American Music Awards and Billboard Music Awards.

Live Performances

Taylor has gone on tour many times, so she is no stranger to performing on stage. Stadiums filled to brim of fans cheering for Taylor.
